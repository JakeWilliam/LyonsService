About Lyons Service & Supply

Introduction:
Est. 1970, after many years in business, Lyons Service and Supply Company has become a diversified company offering a wide range of services.

We continue to serve our loyal customers with great customer service, low lead times and competitive pricing.

Industries served include auto, marine, cabinet, restaurants, hospitals, sheds, military, and many others.
